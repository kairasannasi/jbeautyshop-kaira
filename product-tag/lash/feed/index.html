]]> Here’s a step-by-step guide for using J Beauty Synthetic Clip-on Hair Extensions:

  1. Lay out your extensions or wefts on a flat surface, grouping together extensions of like sizes.
  2.  Part your hair into sections.
  3. Take a perpendicular section of hair from your part down to your ear and tease the roots to create a base to hold the hair extensions.
  4. Take the J Beauty Synthetic Clip-on Hair Extension and use the clip side to attach it to the teased area, securing it in place.
  5. Repeat the process, sectioning off hair and clipping on the hair extensions until you achieve the desired volume and length.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Please keep in mind that J Beauty Synthetic Clip-on Hair Extensions are not heat-resistant, so do not use any heat on the hair extensions.
